Your 14-Day Cooling-Off Right

What is it?

Under EU law, you have the right to cancel any online purchase within 14 days without giving a reason. This is called the "cooling-off period."

When does the period start?

  • RTF Kits & Components: The day you receive the goods
  • Custom Builds: The day you receive the completed drone
  • Digital Goods: Right waived upon download (see below)

How to cancel

  1. Email returns@fpvbuildlab.ie with your name, order number, and clear statement that you wish to cancel
  2. We will confirm receipt within 24 hours
  3. Return the goods within 14 days of cancellation

Condition Requirements by Product

RTF Kits

  • Must be unflown (no prop marks, no dirt, no signs of use)
  • All original packaging intact
  • All seals unbroken
  • All accessories included
  • If flown, value deducted based on condition (up to 100% if crashed)

Components

  • Unopened, original packaging
  • No solder marks, no signs of installation
  • All parts included
  • Opened items subject to restocking fee (20-30%) if resalable

Custom Builds

  • Components only (labour non-refundable)
  • Must be unflown
  • All original component packaging must be returned

Refunds

How refunds work

  • Refund issued within 14 days of receiving returned goods
  • Refund includes standard shipping cost (not premium shipping upgrades)
  • Refund to original payment method
  • You pay return shipping (unless item faulty or our error)

Deductions

  • If product value diminished by handling beyond necessary, we deduct accordingly
  • "Necessary handling" = opening outer box, visual inspection
  • "Beyond necessary" = flying, installing, soldering, crashing
  • Deduction amount based on condition (typically 20-100%)

Refund Timeline

  • Day 1-2: Return delivered
  • Day 3-5: Inspection completed
  • Day 5-7: Refund processed
  • Day 7-10: Refund appears in account (varies by bank)

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I test fly the drone and still return it?

No. Flying constitutes "handling beyond necessary to establish nature, characteristics, and functioning." If you fly it, you have accepted the product. Value deduction applies (up to 100%).

What if I opened the box but didn't fly it?

Opening the outer box for visual inspection is fine. Opening sealed antistatic bags or removing protective films may reduce value.

Who pays return shipping?

You pay for change of mind returns. We pay for faulty/damaged/incorrect items.

How long do I have to return after cancelling?

14 days from the date you notify us of cancellation.

Can I return batteries?

No. Due to dangerous goods regulations, LiPo batteries cannot be returned by post. All battery sales are final.

I crashed my drone, can I return it?

No. Crash damage is not covered by warranty or returns policy.
