Why WEEE Matters

Electrical and electronic equipment (EEE) contains materials that can harm the environment if not disposed of properly. The WEEE Directive ensures:

  • ✅ Valuable materials are recovered and recycled
  • ✅ Hazardous substances are safely treated
  • ✅ Producers pay for recycling (not households)
  • ✅ Reduced environmental impact

Your Responsibilities

When You Buy New

  • You are responsible for recycling your old electrical equipment
  • Do not put WEEE in household waste (bins)
  • Use local authority recycling centres
  • Return to retailer when buying like-for-like

When You Dispose

  • Find your nearest WEEE collection point: www.weeeireland.ie/find-a-recycling-centre/
  • Remove batteries if possible (recycle separately)
  • Take to designated WEEE area (usually at civic amenity sites)
  • Recycling is FREE for household WEEE

Our Free Take-Back Service

Like-for-Like Take-Back

When you buy a new electrical item from us, you can return an equivalent old item for recycling free of charge.

  • Example: Buy a new flight controller → return old flight controller
  • Example: Buy a new drone → return old drone
  • Example: Buy new goggles → return old goggles

How to Use the Service

  1. Package your old item securely
  2. Include note with your name, order number (of new item), and "WEEE TAKE-BACK"
  3. Send to: FPV Build Lab, [Your Address], Dublin, Ireland
  4. We will recycle it responsibly through our WEEE scheme

Note: You pay shipping to us. We cover recycling costs.

Battery Recycling

Separate Collection

Batteries are covered by the Battery Directive and must be recycled separately.

  • LiPo batteries must NEVER go in household waste
  • Discharge completely before recycling (if possible)
  • Tape terminals to prevent short circuits
  • Take to battery collection points (most supermarkets, hardware stores, recycling centres)
  • Find battery recycling: www.weeeireland.ie/battery-recycling/

⚠️ Damaged/Swollen Batteries: Handle with extreme care. Place in fireproof container, contact local recycling centre for special instructions. Do not ship damaged batteries.

Environmental Benefits

By recycling your WEEE, you help:

  • 🌍 Recover precious metals (gold, silver, copper)
  • 🌍 Reduce mining for new materials
  • 🌍 Prevent toxic chemicals entering landfill
  • 🌍 Reduce carbon footprint of manufacturing
  • 🌍 Create jobs in recycling industry

Every tonne of recycled electronics saves approximately 15 tonnes of CO2 emissions.
