7. Dangerous Goods (LiPo Batteries)

⚠️ CRITICAL: Lithium Polymer (LiPo) batteries are Class 9 Dangerous Goods.

7.1 Shipping Requirements

  • LiPos ship ONLY via DHL/UPS Dangerous Goods service.
  • DG surcharge applies (typically €15-€30 extra).
  • Each battery in individual fire-retardant packaging.
  • Terminals protected against short circuit.
  • Class 9 hazard label on outer package.
  • Shipper's Declaration for Dangerous Goods completed.

7.2 Prohibitions

  • LiPos CANNOT be shipped via standard post (An Post, standard DPD, etc.).
  • LiPos CANNOT be shipped to PO boxes.
  • LiPos CANNOT be returned (see Section 9).

7.3 Safety Information

  • Never leave charging LiPos unattended.
  • Use LiPo-safe bag for charging/storage.
  • If battery swells, dispose of safely immediately.
  • Never puncture, crush, or incinerate.
  • Recycle at designated WEEE collection points.

7.4 Damaged Batteries

  • If battery arrives damaged, do NOT accept delivery.
  • Contact us immediately with photos.
  • We will arrange replacement/refund.
