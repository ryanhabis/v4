6. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights:

  1. Right to Access (Art. 15) - Request a copy of your data
  2. Right to Rectification (Art. 16) - Correct inaccurate data
  3. Right to Erasure (Art. 17) - "Right to be forgotten"
  4. Right to Restrict Processing (Art. 18) - Limit how we use your data
  5. Right to Data Portability (Art. 20) - Receive data in machine-readable format
  6. Right to Object (Art. 21) - Object to processing (including marketing)
  7. Right to Withdraw Consent (Art. 7) - Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise your rights: Email dpo@fpvbuildlab.ie with your request. We will respond within 30 days (as required by law).

7. Data Security

  • Encryption: SSL/TLS for all website traffic (HTTPS)
  • Access Control: Only necessary personnel have access to customer data
  • Secure Servers: Hosted in EU data centers
  • Breach Response: We will notify DPC within 72 hours and affected users without delay

8. Data Retention

  • Orders & Invoices: 7 years (Irish tax law requirement)
  • Customer support queries: 2 years after resolution
  • Marketing data: Until consent withdrawn
  • Website logs: 30 days

9. Children's Data

Our website and products are not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you believe we have data from a child under 16, please contact us immediately.

10. International Transfers

Some of our third-party processors (e.g., Google, Stripe) may transfer data outside the EEA. When this occurs, we ensure:

  • The country has an EU adequacy decision, or
  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) are in place, or
  • The processor is Privacy Shield certified (for US transfers)
