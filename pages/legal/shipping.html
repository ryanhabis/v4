📦 Dangerous Goods Shipping Policy

Last Updated: March 2026 | Compliant with IATA Dangerous Goods Regulations 60th Edition & ADR 2023

⚠️ DANGEROUS GOODS CLASS 9

Lithium Polymer (LiPo) batteries are classified as Class 9 Dangerous Goods. They require special handling, packaging, and shipping.

Why This Matters

LiPo batteries contain significant energy and can pose fire risks if damaged or short-circuited. International and EU regulations strictly control their transport. FPV Build Lab is legally required to follow these regulations. Failure to do so can result in:

  • Fines up to €5,000 per incident for individuals
  • Criminal charges for company directors
  • Loss of shipping privileges
  • Invalidation of insurance
  • Danger to transport workers and the public

Our Shipping Process

Step 1: Identification

When you order products containing LiPo batteries, our system flags the order as "Dangerous Goods."

Step 2: Packaging

  • Each battery placed in individual fire-retardant pouch
  • Terminals covered with tape to prevent short circuit
  • Batteries packed in strong outer box with cushioning
  • "Package orientation" arrows on all sides (if required)

Step 3: Labeling

Outer package must display:

  • Class 9 hazard label (diamond, black & white stripes)
  • "Lithium Ion Batteries" handling label
  • CAUTION text with contact number
  • 24-hour emergency contact number

Step 4: Documentation

  • Shipper's Declaration for Dangerous Goods
  • Air waybill marked "Dangerous Goods"
  • Packing list noting battery contents

Step 5: Carrier Selection

We only use couriers with Dangerous Goods accreditation:

  • DHL Dangerous Goods Service
  • UPS Dangerous Goods Service
  • FedEx Dangerous Goods Service (by arrangement)

We NEVER use: An Post, standard DPD, Fastway, or any non-DG courier for batteries.

Prohibited Destinations

We cannot ship LiPo batteries to:

  • PO Boxes (physical address and recipient required)
  • Remote islands with no DG courier service
  • Countries with import bans on lithium batteries (check before ordering)
  • Military APO/FPO addresses (restrictions vary)
  • Some EU countries may have temporary restrictions - we will advise

Before Purchase

  • ✓ Someone 18+ must be available to sign for package

  • ✓ Verify battery size/connector carefully - batteries cannot be returned

After Delivery

  • ✓ Charge only with compatible LiPo charger
  • ✓ Never leave charging unattended
  • ✓ Use LiPo-safe bag for charging/storage
  • ✓ Store at storage voltage (3.8V/cell) if not using

Safety Information

Charging Safety

  • Always use a balance charger designed for LiPo batteries
  • Set correct cell count and charge rate (typically 1C)
  • Never charge above 4.2V per cell
  • Charge in a fireproof container or LiPo bag
  • Never leave charging unattended
  • Keep away from flammable materials

Storage Safety

  • Store at storage voltage (3.8V-3.85V per cell) if not using for >3 days
  • Store in fireproof container
  • Keep away from metal objects that could short terminals
  • Ideal storage temperature: 5-25°C

Disposal

  • Never put LiPos in household waste (bins)
  • Discharge completely before disposal (salt water method for dead cells)
  • Take to local recycling center or WEEE collection point
  • Find nearest: www.weeeireland.ie

Emergency Response

  • If battery catches fire: Use Class D fire extinguisher, sand, or dry powder. Do NOT use water.
  • If battery swells: Stop using immediately, place in fireproof container, discharge outdoors carefully, dispose.
  • If battery leaks: Avoid skin contact, ventilate area, clean with PPE, dispose safely.
