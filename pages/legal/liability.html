4. Product Use Warning

4.1 RTF Kits

  • Always perform a pre-flight check before each flight
  • Test failsafe and range on the ground first
  • Start in a safe, open area away from people and property
  • Use a spotter when flying FPV
  • Never fly beyond line of sight (unless authorized)
  • Respect no-fly zones and privacy

4.2 LiPo Batteries

  • Only use chargers designed for LiPo batteries
  • Never leave charging unattended
  • Charge in a fireproof container or LiPo bag
  • Check for damage before each use
  • Store at storage voltage (3.8V/cell)
  • Dispose of damaged batteries properly
  • Keep away from children and pets

4.3 FPV Goggles

  • Do not walk or move while wearing goggles
  • Take regular breaks to avoid eye strain
  • Not suitable for persons with epilepsy or photosensitivity
  • Keep antennas away from eyes
