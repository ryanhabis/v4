❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use my actual FPV radio?

Yes! Most modern radios (Radiomaster TX16S, TBS Tango 2, etc.) connect via USB and work as a joystick. Older radios may need a simulator cable.

Do I need a gaming PC?

Not necessarily. FPV Freerider runs on integrated graphics. Liftoff and DRL need a basic dedicated GPU (GTX 1050 or better). Check requirements before buying.

Which simulator is most realistic?

VelociDrone is widely considered the most realistic, especially for racing. Liftoff is a close second with better graphics.

Can I play with friends?

Liftoff, DRL, and VelociDrone have multiplayer. Uncrashed and Tryp are single-player only.

How long should I practice?

Most people are comfortable flying after 10-20 hours. Before your first real flight, aim to complete a full lap without crashing in the sim.

Do I need a controller?

Keyboard is technically possible but terrible. Use an Xbox/PlayStation controller or ideally your actual FPV radio.

Are there free simulators?

Yes! DRL has a free trial. FPV Freerider has a free demo. There's also FPV Air 2 free version on some platforms.

Will simulator skills transfer to real life?

Absolutely! Muscle memory, throttle control, and orientation skills all transfer directly. You'll crash less and progress faster.
